My promises to you...

Education

  • Obtain more funding for our schools
  • Make healthier meal options available
  • Improve programs for students with disabilities

Economy

  • Supporting local businesses with networking events, workshops, and mentorship programs
  • Bringing in new business with grants, incentives, and fast-tracked permitting processes
  • Building new apartment complexes
  • Lower sales tax

Infrastructure

  • Safer roads by fixing potholes, repaving roads, adding bike lanes
  • Solar Panels for high-energy buildings
  • Conserving our environment and creating green spaces with trees, parks, and community gardens

Working with the community

  • Going door-to-door to learn what community members truly want
  • Improving transparency and accountability
  • Getting the community involved in projects via surveys and flyers

My Promise! I will work with you!

Ashlyn Guinn
